About the Walk

Harajuku Fashion Walk originally started in Tokyo. It's a colorful and inclusive event where everyone can express themselves through their unique style! Join us as we walk through downtown Vancouver to celebrate Harajuku fashion culture, make new friends, and share kawaii energy with the world!

What's Harajuku Fashion?

Harajuku fashion, originating in Tokyo's Harajuku district, is a vibrant and diverse style characterized by bold colors, playful patterns, and a strong emphasis on individuality and self-expression, often seen as a rebellion against traditional Japanese norms.
